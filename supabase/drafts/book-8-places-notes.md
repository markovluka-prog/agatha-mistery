# Book 8 Places Notes

Best-effort draft with only the strongest story-relevant external location kept.

## Confirmed location

- Mombasa, Kenya

## OCR / scan evidence

- Printed pages 90-91: the text says the ship is due to leave for Mombasa, and the heroes must reach the port before departure.
- This gives Mombasa a clear narrative role: it is the crucial port where the smuggling route may still be intercepted.

## Removed during cleanup

- `Kenya` was removed from the draft as too broad a country-level point for `places`.
- `Oman` was removed as a route endpoint that felt weaker than Mombasa for a map card.
- Indoor spaces, unnamed settings, and transport without a fixed place remain excluded.
